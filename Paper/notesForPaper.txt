Existing Related Projects

Behavioral Program Synthesis - introduces the idea of a "search
driver" which could be useful to us.
http://www.cs.put.poznan.pl/kkrawiec/wiki/uploads/Research/2015GPTP.pdf

Paper by Jerry Swan and others about the shape of fitness landscapes
for an algebraic problem; an attempt to understand *why* certain
search methods do better than others
http://www.worldscientific.com/doi/abs/10.1142/S0218196711006753

Machine learning and automated theorem proving - James Bridge PhD
thesis - focused more on proofs of properties of programs than
traditional mathematical concepts, but some good ideas.
https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/techreports/UCAM-CL-TR-792.pdf


https://acmes.org - relevant conference
